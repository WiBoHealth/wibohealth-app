Glycemic Index (GI)

A scale that shows how quickly food affects blood sugar levels from 0 to 100. Foods with low GI (less than 55) help stabilize blood sugar levels.

Insulin Index (II)

A scale that shows how much insulin the body needs to digest food. Knowing this index is important for diabetics and those who want to control weight.

Understanding Health Indices

Glycemic Index

  • Low (0-55): Slow absorption, ideal for health
  • Medium (56-69): Moderate absorption
  • High (70+): Fast absorption, raises sugar quickly

Insulin Index

  • Low (0-55): Low insulin release
  • Medium (56-69): Moderate insulin release
  • High (70+): High insulin release
